VFX Transition Prompt Builder - 14 nhom cau hoi hien tai

Muc dich:
Danh sach nay gom 14 nhom thong tin dang duoc dung trong form VFX Transition Prompt Builder.
Co the dung tai lieu nay de dua sang cac cong cu AI khac nghien cuu, rut gon, bo sung hoac toi uu lai cau hoi.

1. Thong tin co ban
- Platform: Veo 3 / Kling 2.0 / ca hai...
- Shot length: vi du 5s, 8s, 10s...
- Aspect ratio: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:3, 2.39:1
- Resolution: optional, vi du 4K, 1080p...

2. Input hinh anh
- Frame dau: mo ta ngan anh dau.
- Frame cuoi: mo ta ngan anh cuoi.
- Match layout: Co / Khong / Linh hoat.
- Focus chinh: vat the hoac nhan vat trung tam.
- Diem cong chuyen: vi du line sang, man hinh, den, logo...

3. Y tuong Transition Flow
- Bat dau: Camera va trang thai ban dau.
- Kich hoat: Hieu ung bat dau tu dau, cai gi trigger.
- Bien doi: stretch, explode, dissolve, morph, energy, tunnel.
- Di chuyen chinh: FPV, zoom, xuyen, roi, bay, orbit.
- Reveal: toi -> sang, assemble, emerge, fade.

4. Camera Choreography
- Loai camera: cinematic, FPV, drone, virtual crane.
- Chuyen dong camera: push-in, pull-out, orbit, dive, glide, whip.
- Nhip camera: slow -> fast -> slow, constant, burst.
- Motion dac biet: roll, tilt, drop, acceleration manh, khong.

5. Style hinh anh
- Genre: Cinematic, Trailer, Commercial.
- Mood: Futuristic, High-tech, Luxury, Minimal.
- Realism style: Realistic, Hyper-realistic, Stylized.
- Density: Clean, Dense VFX, Atmospheric.

6. He VFX chinh
- Light trails
- Volumetric light
- Beams
- Glow / bloom
- Energy pulse
- Holographic lines
- Particle dust
- Particle spark
- Particle digital
- Smoke / haze
- Shockwave
- Grid / tech lines

7. VFX phu
- Lens flare
- Motion streak
- Floating dust
- Subtle sparks
- Atmosphere fog

8. Mau sac va Anh sang
- Main color: vi du cyan, deep blue, gold...
- Secondary color: vi du magenta, white, amber...
- Light type: cold, warm, neutral.
- Contrast: low, medium, high.
- Glow intensity: nhe, vua, manh.

9. Nhip va Timing
- Build-up: so giay.
- Acceleration: so giay.
- Reveal: so giay.
- Hit beat: Co / Khong.

10. Muc do Realism
- Ultra realistic
- Cinematic realistic
- Semi-stylized
- Abstract

11. Rang buoc bat buoc
- Giu nguyen: vi du logo, mau thuong hieu, layout...
- Khong duoc thay doi: vi du ti le logo, vi tri text...
- Khong them: vi du text, watermark, nguoi...

12. Negative Prompt
- Negative co dinh:
  - Fake CGI
  - Game-like visuals
  - Qua nhieu particle
  - Blur manh
  - Rung handheld
  - Chay sang qua muc
  - Fantasy qua da
- Negative them: vi du cartoon, anime, low resolution...

13. Reference
- Trailer cong nghe
- Opening event lon
- Sci-fi cinematic
- Automotive spot
- Fashion film
- Reference khac: link YouTube hoac mo ta ngan.

14. Output mong muon
- Prompt tieng Anh
- Co negative prompt
- Viet theo beat
- Ghi chu them: optional, yeu cau dac biet cho prompt.
